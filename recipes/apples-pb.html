Apples And Peanut Butter

Desctiption

This very easy recipe can be eaten at any time of day when you're hungry, can be filling, and it's fast.

Ingredients (Organic if possibe)

  • Apple (green or red)
  • Peanut butter (creamy or crunchy)
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Get bowl or plate then put two or three small (or big!) scoops of peanut butter on there
  2. On the peanut butter sprinkle some cinnamon
  3. Take your apple, bite, then use your spoon to take the peanut butter to eat them together (or dip the apple in the peanut butter if you prefer)
