Blueberry Banana Oatmeal

Description

This bowl of oatmeal recipe is easy to make, quick, and you can modify it by adding or substituting with any fruit, powder, etc.

Ingredients (Organic if possible)

  • Regular oatmeal (if not regular, get ones not high in sugar)
  • Fresh blueberries
  • Fresh sliced banana
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Pour 1/4 cup of natural spring water in pot until boil
  2. Pour the amount of oatmeal you want in a bowl
  3. When water is boiled, pour on top of oatmeal
  4. Put a bunch of bluerries on top
  5. Slice one ripe banana and put on top
  6. Sprinkle a little cinnamon on all this and mix in
